FAQs

Let us show you how your product comes to life.

What bulk discount rates do you offer for wholesalers?

We offer tiered discount rates for wholesalers starting at orders of 50+ units, with greater discounts for larger quantities. Please contact us for a detailed quote.

Can I request sample prints before placing a bulk order?

Yes, we understand the importance of quality assurance and offer sample prints upon request for verified wholesalers.

What is the turnaround time for large orders?

Typically, the turnaround time for bulk orders is 2-3 weeks. This may vary depending on order size and design complexity.

Do you provide custom design services for t-shirt printing?

Our in-house design team can assist in creating custom designs tailored to your branding needs.

Are there any limitations on print design or color choices?

We offer a wide range of colors and support complex designs. However, some special inks or techniques may have limitations which we can discuss in detail.

What is your policy on returns or exchanges for bulk orders?

We adhere to a strict quality control process but in the case of any defects, we offer a returns or exchange policy for all bulk orders.
